I am a final-year PhD student in Stanford's CS department, where I'm fortunate to be advised by Benjamin Van Roy.

The goal of my research is to design algorithms which can continually adapt to changes in dynamic environments. I have been fortunate to be supported by the Stanford Knight Hennessy Fellowship and the NSF GRFP Fellowship. Previously, I was an AI resident at Google Brain. I graduated from Georgia Tech with a Bachelors in Computer Science and minor in Applied Math. At Georgia Tech, I was fortunate to be supported by the Barry Goldwater Scholarship and the Stamps President's Scholarship.

I spend most of my free time playing the tabla. I also enjoy swimming and playing tennis.
